Minestrone

Description

This hearty soup is packed full of delicious vegetables and will be sure to satisfy on a cold day.

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 cup onion, chopped
  • 2 ribs of celery, chopped
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 28 ounce can diced tomatoes
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 2 zucchini, chopped
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • 4 cups tomato juice
  • 1 can kidney beans
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 tablespoon dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme

Directions

  1. Cook the onions and celery in oil over low heat for 15 minutes, covered. Add garlic and cook for five minutes more.
  2. Add the vegetables and let them soften for an additional 15 minutes.
  3. Add remaining ingredients and simmer, uncovered, for at least 45 minutes.
